CGC Custom Label Guide

The CGC Custom Label Series offers a fun and affordable way to elevate graded comics. Featuring fan-favorite characters, iconic brands and the hottest publishers, CGCs custom labels tie the label with the collectible for a dynamic, eye-catching presentation while never obstructing important identification information. Its easy to add a CGC custom label to your comic  just select from all available labels when filling out the CGC online submission form! But dont wait to get your favorites! Most custom labels are only available for a limited time.
